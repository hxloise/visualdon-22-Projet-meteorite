Illuminated sky

"Two young people are peacefully lying in the middle of a field. Immersed in a pleasant calm, the two have their eyes fixed on the sky. Their goal is simple: to see this small comet fragment that brings happiness. "The shooting star". It has now been half an hour since either of them has dared to break the silence, when a small "oh" of admiration is heard. However, the small white line that bars the sky lasts more than a fraction of a second. In addition, it is particularly bright. Some kind of green, or maybe red. Who knows. A few seconds pass and then both marvel at the same time. "Crazy, it must have been a meteorite". Crazy, yes, but is it so rare?"
